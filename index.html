Frequently Asked Questions

💸 Do you offer refunds? +
Since the access code is sent and the customisation window is unlocked specifically for you, we are unable to offer refunds once the code has been issued. We encourage you to view the live demo before purchasing — and reach out on WhatsApp with any questions before you pay. 💖
⏱️ How long does it take to get my code? +
We send your unique access code within 0 to 30 minutes of receiving your payment screenshot on WhatsApp. Once you have the code, you customise and publish instantly — your website goes live the moment you hit Publish.
💰 How do I pay? +
Pay via any UPI app — GPay, PhonePe, Paytm and more — directly on the customize page. Then send the payment screenshot to our WhatsApp. We verify and send your code within 30 minutes. Full payment upfront, no advance/split needed.
🔒 Is my personal content private? +
100% yes — and this is what makes us different. You upload your photos and messages directly into your website yourself. We never ask you to send us your personal content. No middleman. No exposure. Your memories stay completely yours.
🌐 How long does the live site stay active? +
Your live website stays accessible for up to 1 full year from the day you publish it. They can open it, revisit it and share it any time. After 1 year, message us on WhatsApp to discuss renewal.
📱 Will it look good on mobile? +
100% yes — every website is fully mobile responsive and looks stunning on any screen. Since most surprises are opened on a phone, mobile experience is always our top priority.
